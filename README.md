# OrbitPoint

A minimalist arcade game where timing is everything. Control a satellite orbiting a glowing star, tap to reverse direction, and dodge incoming space debris.

## Features

- One-tap controls - tap anywhere to reverse orbit
- Earn coins for every second you survive
- Unlock satellite colors, sun styles, debris colors, and theme packs
- Animated starfield with twinkling stars and shooting stars
- Game Center leaderboards
- Atmospheric background music

## How to Play

1. Your satellite automatically orbits the central star
2. Tap anywhere to reverse your orbital direction
3. Avoid the incoming space debris
4. Survive as long as possible to earn coins
5. Spend coins in the store to customize your game

## Requirements

- iOS 17.0+
- iPhone or iPad

## Building

1. Clone the repository
2. Install [XcodeGen](https://github.com/yonaskolb/XcodeGen): `brew install xcodegen`
3. Run `xcodegen generate`
4. Open `OrbitPoint.xcodeproj` in Xcode
5. Build and run

## Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 2025

OrbitPoint ("the App") is developed by David Bond. This privacy policy explains how the App handles user data.

### Data Collection

OrbitPoint does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App:

- Does not require account creation
- Does not collect personal information
- Does not use analytics or tracking
- Does not access contacts, location, or other device data
- Does not contain advertisements

### Game Center

OrbitPoint integrates with Apple Game Center for leaderboards. Game Center is operated by Apple and subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/). Your Game Center display name and scores are managed by Apple, not by OrbitPoint.

### Local Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

- High scores
- In-game currency balance
- Unlocked cosmetic items
- User preferences (sound, haptics, music volume)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer.

### Children's Privacy

OrbitPoint does not collect any data and is safe for users of all ages.
